About me

a Creative Director and Product Designer, based in Tel-Aviv.
I work mostly with startups and tech companies, designing tools for developers and IT.

My process combines agility and appliance to changing needs, with long-sighted, accumulative value for your product goals and vision, within the scope of daily, local design acts.

I work seamlessly with developers, code my own designs as part of my workflow, often start working directly in the browser and moving back to graphic assets for documentation.

When not designing things, I illustrate, animate, draw from observation, surf the waves of the closest ocean and take frequent study vacations in the 15th century (it's closer than people think).
